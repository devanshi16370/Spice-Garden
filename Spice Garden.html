OUR STORY

Serving Authentic Flavors Since 2008

SpiceGarden was started by Chef Ramesh with a dream to bring real, homestyle Indian cooking to every table. What started as 10 seats has grown into a city favorite.

Every dish is cooked fresh daily with traditional recipes passed down through generations, using hand-ground spices and local ingredients.

  • ✔️100% fresh ingredients sourced locally every morning
  • ✔️No artificial colors or preservatives used
  • ✔️Vegetarian and vegan-friendly options available
  • ✔️Cozy family-friendly dining atmosphere
